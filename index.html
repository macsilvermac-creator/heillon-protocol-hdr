01
The Protocol

AI without proof
is just an opinion.

Current AI systems make decisions with zero verifiable trace of why, when, or under whose authority they acted. HEILLON changes this fundamentally.

Our protocol wraps every consequential action — AI or human — in an immutable cryptographic record that proves its legitimacy: the authority chain, the temporal context, and the intention behind it.

This is not compliance software. This is civilizational infrastructure — the layer that makes AI governance possible.
