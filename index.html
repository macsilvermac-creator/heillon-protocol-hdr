05 Five Pillars
P-01 · SOVEREIGN TIME
Tamper-proof temporal anchoring
Every event sealed to a verifiable, cryptographically immutable timestamp. No retroactive manipulation possible.
P-02 · IMMUTABLE LEDGER
Cryptographic chain of custody
Every action recorded with full context, permanently and irreversibly, via SHA-256 hash chain.
P-03 · FORMAL AUTHORITY
Verifiable mandate binding
Actions bound to cryptographically verifiable authority claims. Command chain always traceable.
P-04 · PRIOR INTENTION
Declared before execution
Intentions sealed into the ledger before acting. Creates an unbreakable link between purpose and action.
P-05 · NLO LEVELS
Legitimacy scoring system
Structured quantification of decision sovereignty — certifiable, auditable, comparable across contexts.

# Every decision passes through:

Z  Zone        → institutional domain + boundaries
J  Jurisdiction → cryptographic authority verification
M  Mandate     → prior intention sealed to ledger
H  HEILLON     → execution + constitutional hash seal
K  Knowledge   → NLO level certification + audit record

# Output: verifiable, reconstructable proof
# Standard: SHA-256 · Hash chain · RFC 3161 timestamps
