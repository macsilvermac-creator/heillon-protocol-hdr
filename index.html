01
The Problem

The Governance
Gap.

AI systems now trigger workflows, execute transactions, and make operational decisions — at machine speed. But institutional governance was designed for humans.

Institutions cannot reliably verify:
  • Who authorized the decision
  • Under which constraints it was executed
  • Whether policy boundaries were respected
  • In which operational context it occurred
Computational Legitimacy

The capacity of a system to prove that a decision was authorized, executed within policy boundaries, and can be reconstructed and audited at any future point.

The HEILLON Answer

A Decision Verification Layer embedded between autonomous systems and institutional infrastructure — making legitimacy a computable property of every execution.
